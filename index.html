Research Interests

Denis Khryashchev is a Ph.D student at the Graduate Center of the City University of New York. Previously he got his master’s degree at the Center of Urban Science and Progress at the New York University in applied data science and urban informatics where his main focus was on applying machine learning techniques on urban data sets of the city agencies of New York City. His main research interests are developing new algorithms and approaches for time series forecasting and analysis, and big data processing. He has made a contribution to 10 articles overall.
